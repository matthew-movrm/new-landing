Frequently Asked Questions

What makes MOVRM different from running apps like Strava or Nike Run Club?

MOVRM prioritises motivation and mindset over metrics. It’s about running together – even remotely.

Do I need to be experienced?

Not at all. MOVRM is specifically designed for new and intermediate runners looking for structure and support.

Can I host a group if I’m not a running coach?

Absolutely. You just need the desire to motivate others — we’ll provide the tools to help.

Do I need to run exactly when the prompt says?

Nope. You’ll have 24 hours to complete it — just try to stay close to the suggested time.
